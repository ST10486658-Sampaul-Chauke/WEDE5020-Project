Our Story

With over 7 years of experience in the electrical industry, Maeddys Electrical has established itself as a leading provider of solar panel installation services in Limpopo and surrounding areas.

We specialize in delivering high-quality, reliable solar energy solutions for residential homes, commercial buildings, retail shops, and bakeries. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for sustainable energy solutions.

To provide affordable, reliable, and sustainable solar energy solutions that help our customers reduce their carbon footprint while saving on electricity costs.

Our Vision

To be the leading solar installation company in South Africa, contributing to a cleaner and more sustainable future for all.
